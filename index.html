01 Web Design / Development

You barely have enough time to handle the daily operations of your independent cafe, restaurant, or bed & breakfast, let alone worry about building a bespoke website. In an ever-evolving digital landscape, you want your website to reflect your physical space and make an impactful first impression. I’ll help you create a custom-coded website that connects your small hospitality business with your guests.

Typically Includes

  • Strategic single or multi page custom-coded websites
  • Custom creation to fit your unqiue brand
  • Using CSS, HTML & JavaScript
  • Mobile friendly & responsive design
  • Basic SEO optimisation
  • Fast & secure build
  • Analytics setup
  • Cross-browser compatibility testing
  • Custom Content Management System setup, for easy updates
  • Post-launch support

Potential add-ons

  • Ongoing Maintenance support
  • Booking engine integration
  • Social Media integration
  • Food & Restaurant Photography / content creation
  • Image Sourcing

Process

  • Strategy - Understanding your goals, vision, style and brand is important to create a website that speaks to your ideal audience. We dive deep into getting to the root of what you are trying to achieve, through a questionnaire and a strategy call.
  • Conceptualisation - We'll walk through moodboards and create a low-fidelity wireframe to bring your ideas and vision to life, making sure that we move forward with a cohesive visual style for your online home.
  • Creation - I use an approved wireframe / design to build a custom-coded website, completing it with a user-friendly CMS that you can update yourself.
  • Delivery - Crafted with your goals in mind, you will receive your final website, uniquely tailored to you. With your new website, we'll have a thorough handover, to make sure you feel prepared to use your new online home with confidence. Time to celebrate!

Timeline

A website can take anywhere between 2 to 12 weeks, depending on the complexity of features required.

*Bespoke quotes - starting at €1200 for an essential website of 1-2 pages.
