about

Ciao, I'm Tatjana & I believe every small impactful business deserves to have their creations seen. So, if the thought of your food and services evoking smiles makes you twirl in your kitchen or back office, I'm thrilled to have you here.

After a decade of experience in the hospitality industry, and having grown up in Namibia, a land of vast nature and incredible wildlife, two things have always been important to me:

  1. Service excellence and delighting others through memorable experiences
  2. Leaving a positive impact in our communities / wherever we go

Hospitality, to me, has always been about community and sharing beautiful memories together. My experience in the industry has allowed me to create some of the most beautiful in-person experiences, through long hours behind the scenes, and relentless dedication.

Now, I’m bringing that dedication in helping small, independent business owners by providing strategic website and food photography services to enhance their online presence and support their journey to not only continue making a mark in the heart of their guests, but fostering change in their community and environment.

The Process

Strategy

This initial call is all about understanding you & your goals, your style, and your vision. We'll discuss your aspirations, and the intricacies of your unique business. This is your chance to ask any questions you may have. A little strategy sessiion will help with discussing your site's structure, goals, ideal client and the visual direction for your online home. After finding understanding your goals and business I’ll work on how we can best strategise the website or photography to speak directly to your ideal guests.

Creation

For websites, I'll use HTML, CSS & Javascript to build a custom-coded website, complete with a user-friendly CMS that you can update yourself.

For photography, I’ll be capturing your imagery, giving updates along the way. Once the photos are taken, you'll receive a gallery to select the final images for editing.

Delivery

Crafted with your goals in mind, it's time to celebrate! You’ll receive your final images or website and a proper handover. You’ll receive 1-2 weeks of support to address any reasonable needs that arise. I'm here to ensure you're fully prepared for this new digital journey.
