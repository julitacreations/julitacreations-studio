Ciao, I'm Tatjana

If the thought of your food and services evoking smiles makes you twirl in your kitchen or back office, I'm thrilled to have you here!

Having grown up in Namibia, surrounded by nature and incredible wildlife, two things have always been important to me:

  1. Service excellence and delighting others through memorable experiences
  2. Leaving a positive impact in our communities / wherever we go

With a Bachelor in International Hospitality Management and a decade of experience in the industry, hospitality to me, has always been about community and creating beautiful memories. My time in the industry has allowed me to create some of the most beautiful in-person moments.

Now, I’m bringing that dedication in helping small, independent business owners by providing strategic website and food photography services to enhance their online presence and support their journey to not only continue making a mark in the heart of their guests, but fostering change in their community and environment.

Driven to make an impact. One dish at a time. One experience at a time.
