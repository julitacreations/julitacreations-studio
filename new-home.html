01 Web Design / Development

You barely have enough time to handle the daily operations of your cafè, restaurant, or bed & breakfast, let alone worry about building a bespoke website. I understand that. I've worked in hospitality long enough to know what your days actually look like.
In an ever-evolving digital landscape, you want your website to reflect your physical space and make an impactful first impression. I don’t try to make your business fit into a template. Your website will be custom coded and shaped around your brand and the experience you want your guests to have before they even walk throuhg your door.

Typically Includes

  • Strategic single or multi page custom-coded websites
  • Home that looks and feels like your business — not a template
  • Custom-built for the way your guests move through it — on all devices
  • Basic SEO optimisation
  • Fast, secure, and reliable, so it never lets you down mid-service
  • Tested accross browsers and devices
  • Custom Content Management System setup, for easy updates
  • Post-launch support, so you're not left figuring it out alone

Potential add-ons

  • Ongoing Maintenance support, so you don't have to do it all alone
  • Booking engine integration
  • Food & Restaurant Photography / content creation
  • Image Sourcing, for when you don't have enough images of your own yet

Process

  • Strategy - Understanding your goals, vision, style and brand is important to create a website that speaks to your ideal audience. We dive deep into getting to the root of what you are trying to achieve, making sure your guest journey is thought of at each step of the process.
  • Conceptualisation - We'll walk through moodboards and create a low-fidelity wireframe to bring your ideas and vision to life, making sure that we move forward with a cohesive visual style for your online home - because your guest journey starts online.
  • Creation - I create a fast, reliable custom custom-coded website, completing it with a user-friendly CMS that you can update yourself.
  • Delivery - Created with your goals in mind, you will receive your final website, tailored to your guests. With your new website, we'll have a thorough handover, to make sure you feel prepared to use your new online home with confidence. Time to celebrate!

Timeline

A website can take anywhere between 5 to 12 weeks, depending on the complexity of features required.

*Bespoke quotes - starting at €1200 for an essential website of 2-3 pages.
